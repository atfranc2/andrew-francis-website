About Me

Andrew was born and raised in Cleveland, Ohio where his love of the environment and technology blossomed. He followed his passions when he attended the University of CIncinnati where he earned a double Bachelors of Science in Environmental Engineering and Environmental Science.

While studying at the University of Cincinnati, Andrew stumbled upon his passion for working with data and developing software tooling. Once again, Andrew followed his passion and attended the Institute of Advanced Analytics (IAA), where he received his Masters of Science in Data Science. While at the IAA, Andrew worked with Wake Forest Baptist Medical Center to create an analytics workflow for identifying factors predicting heart disease in a NMR metabolomics dataset.

Andrew currently works at the North Carolina Joint Underwriting Association (NCJUA) as a Software Engineer. At NCJUA, Andrew works with an agile team of engineers to design, develop, and maintain insurance applications. He is currently the lead engineer on an internal productivity management software used to optimize and enhance insurance workflows.

In his freetime, when he is not developing his own applications or watching Code With Mosh videos, Andrew enjoys baking sourdough bread using his home-grown yeast culture. He may be spotted tending to his herb garden trying to harvest the best herbs to bake his next loaf of bread. Andrew can also be found pedaling his bicycle around town hunting for the best coffee beans to take home to his French press.
